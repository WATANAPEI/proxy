LESSON16 THE VERB TENSES

1. KU TENSE (PRESENT TENSE)

  • 1. Do you eat sima every day?
  • 2. Do you go to work[work place] at siz o'clock?
  • 3. Do you like sima?
  • 4. Do you like to eat sima?
  • 5. Does he play football?
  • 1. Kasi mukulya sima dazi lililose?
  • 2. Kasi mukuluta ku ntchito 6 koloko?
  • 3. Kasi mukutemwa sima?
  • 4. Kasi mukutemwa kulya sima?
  • 5. Kasi wakusewera bola?

POSITIVE/NEGATIVE STATEMENT ANSWERS

  • 1.
    • Yes, I eat sima every day.
    • No, I don't eat sima every day.
  • 2.
    • Yes, I go to work at six o'clock.
    • No, I don't go to work at six o'clock.
  • 3.
    • Yes, I like sima.
    • No, I don't like sima.
  • 4.
    • Yes, I like to eat sima.
    • No, I don't like to eat sima.
  • 5.
    • Yes, He plays football.
    • No, he doesn't play football.
  • 1.
    • Enya nkhulya sima dazi lililose.
    • Nkhulya sima dazi lililose yayi.
  • 2.
    • Enya nkhuluta ku nthito 6 koloko.
    • Nkhuluta ku nthito 6 koloko yayi.
  • 3.
    • Enya nkhutemwa sima.
    • Nkhutemwa sima yayi.
  • 4.
    • Enya nkhutemwa kulya sima.
    • Nkhutemwa kulya sima yayi.
  • 5.
    • Enya, wakusewera bola.
    • Wakusewera bola yayi.

2. KU TENSE (PRESENT CONTINUOUS TENSE)

  • 1. Are you eating sima now?
  • 2. Do you want[Are you wanting] water?
  • 3. Do you want[Are you wanting] to drink water
  • 4. Does he want[is he wanting] water?
  • 1. Kasi mukulya sima sono?
  • 2. Kasi mukukhumba maji?
  • 3. Kasi mukukhumba kumwa maji?
  • 4. Kasi wakukhumba maji?

POSITIVE/NEGATIVE STATEMENT ANSWERS

  • 1.
    • Yes, I am eating sima now.
    • No, I am not eatiing sima now.
  • 2.
    • Yes, I want water.
    • No, I don't want water.
  • 3.
    • Yes, I want to drink water.
    • No, I don't want to drink water.
  • 4.
    • Yes, he wants water.
    • No, he doesn't want water.
  • 1.
    • Enya nkhulya sima sono.
    • Nkhulya sima sono yayi.
  • 2.
    • Enya nkhukhumba maji.
    • Nkhukhumba maji yayi.
  • 3.
    • Enya nkhukhumba kumwa maji.
    • Nkhukhumba kumwa maji yayi.
  • 4.
    • Enya wakukhumba maji.
    • Wakukhumba maji yayi.

3. KA TENSE (PAST TENSE)

  • 2. Did they eat sima yesterday?
  • 3. Did Violet eat sima yesterday?
  • 1. Kasi mukalya sima mayilo?
  • 2. Kasi wakalya sima mayilo?
  • 3. Kasi Violet wakalya sima mayilo?

POSITIVE/NEGATIVE STATEMENT ANSWERS

  • 1.
    • Yes, I ate sima yesterday.
    • No, I did not eat sima yesterday.
  • 2.
    • Yes, they ate sima yesterday.
    • No, they didn't eat sima yesterday.
  • 3.
    • Yes, Violet ate sima yesterday.
    • No, Violet didn't eat sima yesterday.
  • 1.
    • Enya nkhalya sima mayilo.
    • Nkhalya sima mayilo yayi.
  • 2.
    • Enya wakalya sima mayilo.
    • wakalya sima mayilo yayi.
  • 3.

4. RECENT PAST TENSE

  • 1. Have you eaten sima today?
  • 2. Have we played football today?
  • 3. Has Violet played football today?
  • 1. Kasi mwalya sima muhanyawuno?
  • 2. Kasi tasewera bola muhanyawuno?
  • 3. Kasi Violet wasewera bola muhanyawuno?

POSITIVE/NEGATIVE STATEMENT ANSWERS

  • 1.
    • Yes, I have eaten sima today.
    • No, I have not eaten sima today.
  • 2.
    • Yes, we have played football today.
    • No, we haven't played football today.
  • 3.
    • Yes, Violet has played football today.
    • No, Violet hasn't played football today.
  • 1.
    • Enya nalya sima muhanyawuno.
    • Nalya sima muhanyawuno yayi.
  • 2.
    • Enya tasewera bola muhanyawuno.
    • Tasewera bola muhanyawuno yayi.
  • 3.
    • Enya Violet wasewera bola muhanyawuno.
    • Violet wasewera bola muhanyawuno yayi.

5. ZAMU TENSE

  • 1. Will you play football tomorrow?
  • 2. Will Violet drink water tomorrow?
  • 3. Will she go to Brantyre next neek?
  • 1. Kasi muzamusewera bola namachero?
  • 2. Kasi Violet wazamumwa maji namachero?
  • 3. Kasi wazamuluta ku Brantyre sabata yikwiza?

POSITIVE/NEGATIVE STATEMENT ANSWERS

  • 1.
    • Yes, I will play football tomorrow.
    • No, I will not play football tomorrow.
  • 2.
    • Yes, Violet will drink water tomorrow.
    • No, Violet will not drink water tomorrow.
  • 3.
    • Yes, she will go to Brantyre next week.
    • No, she will not go to Brantyre next week.
  • 1.
    • Enya nizamusewera bola namachero.
    • Nizamusewera bola namachero yayi.
  • 2.
    • Enya Violet wazamumwa maji namachero.
    • Violet wazamumwa maji namachero yayi.
  • 3.
    • Enya wazamuluta ku Brantyre sabata yikwiza.
    • wazamuluta ku Brantyre sabata yikwiza yayi.

IMMEDIATE AND DISTANT FUTURE TENSE

  • 1. Will you eat sima at 7 o'clock today?
  • 2. Will she go to Brantyre next week?
  • 1. Kasi mulyenge sima 7 koloko muhanyawuno?
  • 2. Kasi walutenge ku Blantyre sabata yikwiza?

POSITIVE/NEGATIVE STATEMENT ANSWERS

  • 1.
    • Yes, I will eat sima at 7 o'clock.
    • No, I will not eat sima at 7 o'clock.
  • 2.
    • Yes, she will go to Blantyre next week.
    • No, she will not go to Blantyre next week.
  • 1.
    • Enya nilyenge sima muhanyawuno.
    • Nilyenge sima yayi muhanyawuno.
  • 2.
    • Enya walutenge ku Brantyre sabata yikwiza.
    • Walutenge ku Brantyre sabata yikwiza yayi.
