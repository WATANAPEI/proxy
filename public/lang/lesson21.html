LESSON21 WORKING WITH CAN

ngakwaniska[can manage]

'nga' works well with Positive statements and Question forms. But in the negative form, if used, it implies 'refusal'. 'ngakwaniska' should therefore be used in the negative.

NOTE: 'ngakwaniska' can also perfectly work with: Question, Positive and negative forms without any problem.

  • 1. Can you come tomorrow?
  • 2. I can come tomorrow.
  • 3. I cannot come tomorrow.
    • - [Refusal to ____].
    • - [Not able to ____].
  • 1. Kasi Mungaza namachero?
  • 2. Ningaza namachero.
  • 3.
    • - Ningaza yayi namachero.
    • - Ningakwa.
