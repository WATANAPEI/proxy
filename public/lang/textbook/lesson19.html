LESSON19 DOING AN ACTION FOR OR ON BEHALF OF SOMEONE/SOMETHING

To show this in Chitumbuka, it is the verb that changes its form to reflect that action. The verb either changes to ___IRA or ___ERA forms.

THE NORMAL VERBS FOR/ON BEHALF OF

  • 1. Cook Phika
  • 2. Buy gula
  • 3. Fix/clean nozga
  • 4. Drive yendeska
  • 5. He built the house for us.
  • 1. phikira
  • 2. gulira
  • 3. nozgera
  • 4. yendeskera
  • 5. Wakatizengera nyumba.

PRACTICE

  • 1. She is fixing the car for me.
  • 2. We are buying bananas for him.
  • 1. Wakuniznozgera galimoto.
  • 2. Tikumugulirantochi.
