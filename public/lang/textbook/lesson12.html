LESSON12 DIALOGUE

DIALOGUE

  • A: Are you hungry?
  • B:
    • - I am hungry.
    • - I am not hungry.
  • A: Are you tired?
  • B:
    • - I am tired.
    • - I am not tired.
  • A: Muli na njala?
  • B:
    • - Nili na njala.
    • - Nili na njala yayi.
  • A: Mwavuka?
  • B:
    • - Navuka.
    • - Navuka yayi.
