LESSON20 WORKING WITH SHOULD

SHOULD

Should in Chitumbuka changes the verb to end with vowel e, but 'shouldn't' does not change.

  • 1. You should drink beer a little.
  • 2. You shouldn' come tomorrow.
  • 3. Should I go now?
  • 1. Mumwe mowa pachoko.
  • 2. Kwiza yayi namachero.
  • 3. Nilute sono?
