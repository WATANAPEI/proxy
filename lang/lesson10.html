LESSON10 INTERROGATIVES

1. WHAT = VICHI, VI, CHI

  • What did you eat yesterday?
  • Kasi mukalya vichi mayilo?

2. WHO = NJANI, NJA

  • Who is cooking?
  • Kasi wakuphika ni njani?

3. WHERE = NKHUNI, KOCHI

  • Where do you stay?
  • Kasi mukukhala nkhuni?

4. WHEN = PAWULI

  • When will you go to Blantyre?
  • Kasi muzamuluta pawuli ku Blantyre?

5. WHY = CHIFUKWA ULI

  • Why are you crying?
  • Chifukwa uli mukulira?/ Kasi chifukwa uli mukulira?

6. WHAT TIME = NYENGO WULI

  • What time do you sleep?
  • Kasi mukugona nyengo uli?
