LESSON22 IMPORTANAT CONJUNCTIONS

1. AFTER - PALA

  • After eating breakfast, I go to work[Used with the present tenses]
  • After eating breakfast, I went to work[Used with the past tenses]
  • After eating breakfast, I will go to work[Used with the future tenses]
  • Pala nalya breakfast nkhuluta ku ntchito.
  • Pala nkhalya breakfast, nkhaluta ku ntchito.
  • Pala nizamulya breakfast, nizamuluta ku ntchito.

2. BEFORE - PAMBERE

  • Before I sleep, I read books.
  • Pambere nindagone nkhuwerenga mabuku.

3. BUT - KWENI

  • I don't like cooking, but I like eating.
  • Nkhutemwa kuphika yayi, kweni Nkhutemwa kulya.

4. THEREFORE/SO - NTHEWULA

  • He is sick therefore he will not come.
  • Wakuluwala, nthewula wazenge yayi.

5. BECAUSE - CHIFUKWA

  • She is sleeping because she is tired.
  • Wakugona chifukwa wavuka.
