LESSON02 GREETINGS

GENERAL GREETINGS

  • A: Hello
  • B: Thanks
  • A: How are you?
  • B: I am good, I don't know you?
  • A: I am good too, thanks
  • B: Thanks very much
  • A: We are grateful
  • B: We are grateful
  • A: Monile.
  • B: Yewo.
  • A: Muli uli?
  • B: Niili makola kwali imwe?
  • A: Naneso nili makola, yewo.
  • B: Yewo chomene.
  • A: Tawonga.
  • B: Tawonga.
  • A: I am a little well
  • B: Sorry
  • A: Thanks
  • A: Nili makola pachoko.
  • B: Phepani.
  • A: Yewo
  • A: How is Violet?
  • B: Violet is well.
  • A: Violet wali uli?
  • B: Violet wali makola.

MORNING GREETINGS

  • A: How have you woken up?
  • B: I have woken up well, how about you?
  • A: Me too, I have woken up well, thanks.
  • B: Thanks.
  • A: Mwawuka uli?
  • B: Nawuka makola, kwali imwe?
  • A: Naneso nawuka makola, yewo.
  • B: Yewo.

AFTERNOON GREETINGS

  • A: How have you spent time?
  • B: I have spent time well, what about you?
  • A: Me too, I have spent time well, thanks.
  • B: Thanks.
  • A: Mwatandala uli?
  • B: Natandala makola, kwali imwe?
  • A: Naneso natandala makola, yewo.
  • B: Yewo.

CASUAL GREETINGS

  • A: How is home?
  • B: Home is well
  • A: Kunyumba kuli uli?
  • B: Kunyumba kuli makola.
