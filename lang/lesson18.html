LESSON18

When verbs immediately pass on their action to the object, the object carries a pronoun that reflects the Noun class, or group where it belongs. It also reflects the singular, or plural form of the object/s

EXAMPLES

  • 1. I saw you.
  • 2. I saw him/her.
  • 3. I saw them.
  • 4. He saw me.
  • 5. He saw us.
  • 6. He saw it[chitenje].
  • 7. He saw them[chitenje].
  • 1. Nkhakuwona.
  • 2. NkhaWawona.
  • 3. Nkhawawona.
  • 4. wakaniwona.
  • 5. wakatiwona.
  • 6. wakachiwona.
  • 7. wakaziwona.

PRACTICE

  • 1. She teaches us chitumbuka.
  • 2. We like you.
  • 3. I bought it [chitenje] at the market.
  • wakutisambizga chitumbuka.
  • Tikuku temwa.
  • Nhkachigula ku mosika.
