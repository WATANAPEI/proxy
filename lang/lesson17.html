LESSON17

THE VERB "LI"

The verb Li is a verb to be. It has two main functions.
[1] To show posession
[2]To show Location

[1] TO SHOW POSESSTION

  • Do you have money?
  • Yes I have money.
  • No I don't have money.
  • He has money.
  • I had money.
  • Kasi muli na ndalama?
  • Enya nili na ndalama.
  • Yayi, nilije ndalama./Nili na ndalama yayi.
  • Wali na ndalama.
  • Nkhawa na ndalama.

[2] TO SHOW LOCATION

  • Where are you?
  • I am at the bank.
  • I am not at the bank.
  • Chitenje is at home.
  • Reserve bank is in city center.
  • Muli nkhuni?/ Mukawa nkhuni?
  • Nili ku bank.
  • Nili ku bank yayi.
  • Chitenje chili ku nyumba.
  • Reserve bank yili ku city center.
